SpaceUp BCN Registration

Hurry up! The first 20 participants to register completely will receive a special present!

The registration fee for SpaceUp Barcelona will be 35€ and you only need to follow these 2 steps:

  1. Register via Eventbrite at https://www.eventbrite.es/e/entradas-spaceup-barcelona-28041215029

  2. Make the 35€ payment via bank transfer at the following bank account:
    ES83 1465 0120 35 1725888785.
    Please state SpaceUpBCN and your full name.
