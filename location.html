Location

Venue

SpaceUp Barcelona will take place at the School of Telecommunications and Aerospace Engineering in Castelldefels. At the same campus the ESA Business Incubation Center is located.

The complete address is:

EETAC – Escola d’Enginyeria de Telecomunicació i Aeroespacial de Castelldefels (Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya)
Esteve Terradas, 7.
08860 Castelldefels
41° 16' 31" N 
1° 59' 13" E

How to get there by car?

If you come from highway C-32 (to Tarragona), get out at exit number 46 (Castelldefels Centro).

From highway C-31 it’s exit number 31 (Canal Olímpic).

There is a free parking in the exterior of the park, right next to the main entry.

How to get there by train?

Train lines R2 and R2Sud communicate Barcelona city center with Castelldefels. Then, it’s a quick walk to the venue.

For schedules and more information you can access this website or take a look at this PDF file.

How to get there by bus?

The following buses connect Castelldefels with Barcelona and the airport:

  • Bus 94
  • Bus 95
  • Bus 97
  • Bus 99 (Airport T1)
