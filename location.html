Location

Venue

SpaceUp Barcelona will take place at the headquarters of GTD System & Software Engineering.

The complete address is:

GTD Barcelona
Passeig de Garcia Fària, 17
08005 Barcelona
41° 24' 2.18" N 
2° 12' 34.51" E

How to get there by car?

From the airport (30 min), get on C-31 and then B-10. Take exit 23 from B-10 and continue on Av. Del Litoral. Drive to Passeig de Garcia Faria.

There is a free parking in the vicinity (5 min walk), next to the beach.

How to get there by public transportation from the city center?

There are two main options to arrive to GTD from the city center (Plaça Catalunya):

  • Take the H16 bus from "Pl-Catalunya-Portal de l’Àngel" station to "Jonquera-Taulat" station and walk about 3 min to the site.
  • Take the L4 subway line from "Urquinaona" station to "Poblenou" station and walk about 10 min to the site.

How to get there by public transportation from the airport?

There are also multiple ways to arrive to GTD from El Prat airport:

  • Take the A1 or A2 bus line from the airport to Pl.Espanya-FGC (direction: "Pl. Catalunya-Fontanella"), then the H16 bus line to "Jonquera-Taulat" station (direction: "Forum"), and finally walk about 3 min to GTD.
  • Take the A1 or A2 bus line from the airport to Pl. Espanya-FGC, walk 3 min to Pl. Espanya, take the L1 subway line from "Pl. Espanya" station to "Urquinaona" station (direction: "Fondo"), then take the L4 subway line from "Urquinaona" station to "Poblenou" station (direction: "La Pau"), and walk 10 min to GTD.
